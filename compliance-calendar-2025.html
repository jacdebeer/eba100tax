üí° Best Practices

Calendar Management

  • Set reminders: 5 days before each deadline
  • Early submission: Submit returns 2-3 days early
  • Backup dates: Have alternative submission plans
  • Year planning: Plan major compliance activities annually

Record Keeping

  • Digital copies: Scan and backup all documents
  • Monthly filing: File documents immediately after transactions
  • Annual archive: Archive completed tax year documents
  • Access control: Ensure documents are easily accessible

Professional Support

  • Tax practitioner: Engage professional for complex matters
  • Bookkeeper: Monthly bookkeeping for accurate records
  • Annual review: Professional tax planning sessions
  • Training: Stay updated on tax law changes

üö® High-Risk Penalty Areas

  • PAYE Submissions: R250-R16,000 monthly penalties
  • VAT Returns: R250 per month plus 10% interest
  • Income Tax Returns: R250 per month for individuals
  • EMP501 Reconciliation: R250 per month plus disputes
  • Late Payments: 10% annual interest on all outstanding amounts
